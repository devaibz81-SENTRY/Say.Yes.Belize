Our Story

It began with a shared umbrella on a rainy Tuesday in the heart of the city. What was meant to be a short commute turned into a three-hour conversation over artisan coffee and old books.

From hiking the misty peaks of the Pacific Northwest to quiet Sunday mornings in our sunlit kitchen, every moment has been a chapter in a story we never want to end. Two years later, under a canopy of stars, we decided to make our forever official.
