A collection of moments, captured and kept.

It started in a small café on a rainy Tuesday. No grand gestures, just two people realizing the world felt a little quieter, a little clearer, when they were together.

"We wanted this day to feel like a scanned memory. Not a perfect production, but a physical record of the people we love."
