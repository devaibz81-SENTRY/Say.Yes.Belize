VOLUME I — THE BEGINNING

A Symphony of Two Souls.

"In the silence of a Parisian morning, two paths converged into a singular, eternal narrative."

Captured in Paris, 2023

ESTABLISHED MMXXIV

It began not with a grand gesture, but with the subtle rustle of parchment in a quiet library on the Rive Gauche. Alexander was immersed in the works of 18th-century cartography, while Beatrice was sketching the intricate moldings of the ceiling. It was a chance encounter fueled by a shared reverence for the old world—a mutual understanding that beauty lies in the details often overlooked by the modern eye.

As the seasons shifted from the golden warmth of autumn to the crisp clarity of a Parisian winter, their conversations evolved from artistic theory to the intimate map of their own lives. Every dinner at Le Comptoir was a new chapter; every stroll across the Pont des Arts was a verse in a poem they were writing together.

The proposal was as understated and intentional as their first meeting. No fireworks, no audience—just a quiet promise made beneath the ancient oaks of a private estate in Provence. It was a commitment to a life built on the foundations of grace, intellectual curiosity, and an unwavering devotion to one another.
